What is a Coach?

A Coach is:

  • A partner for a period of time to come along side of you to help you get to where you would like to go, like a stagecoach taking you to your destination.
  • Is more focused on your growth and success than you are
  • Listens and believes in you, your value, and your potential in life (Believes in you before you believe in yourself)
  • Helps you discover your potential and helps you move forward with it
  • Help you see and feel your dreams and then helps you practically move toward them

Coaches can be of great service to people that are:

  • Successful
  • Take responsibility for their lives
  • Invest in themselves
  • Work hard for results every week
  • Are dissatisfied with some aspect of their life or business
    • * Feel stuck or frozen in life, and want to move on or through a situation and feel good about their life contribution and progress

* A Coach Is Not:

      A person who focuses on addressing past issues or that gives you advice, like a: teacher, trainer, facilitator, counselor, therapist, consultant, advisor, mentor, or good friend.

A Coach Can Help You or Your Business:

  • Become a stronger person
  • Go somewhere you have not been able to get on your own
  • Get results faster
  • Re-ignite your hope, drive, and belief in yourself, your future, and your dreams

"A Coach is just the partner for you during this time, to help you move forward. "
